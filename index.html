How Many Regular Helium Balloons Would It Take to Lift Someone?

Helium has a lifting force of 1 (1.05) gram per litre. Hydrogen has a force of 1.13 gram per litre.

A normal balloon at an amusement park might be 30 centimetres (about 1 foot) in diameter.

Volume is calculated as 4/3pi*r3.







In order to ascend, a larger balloon or more balloons should be selected. In order to decend, a smaller balloon should be selected.
